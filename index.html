How to Play

🎰

Core Objective

Start with a balance, choose casino games, win money, use items, and reach the target goal before going broke or running out of time.

The goal scales around your run, so you are always chasing the next risky target.

When you hit the goal, you can cash out and save your score to the leaderboard.

Casino Games

Each game has its own risk style and item support.

🟡 Plinko

Drop a ball through pegs. Win based on the slot multiplier. Lose if it lands on 0x. Affected by Weighted Ball and RoseKat’s Magnum Opus.

🎡 Roulette

Pick red, black, or green, then spin. Match the color to win. Roulette Magnet and EJM’s Record Vinyl help here.

🚀 Crash

Start the rocket multiplier and cash out before it crashes. Wait too long and you lose the bet.

🃏 Blackjack

Beat the dealer without going over 21. Hit, stand, and use card-related items like Dealer’s Peek Card or Thottie’s Jester Hat.

🐘 Elephant River

Move the elephant forward across river lanes. Cash out or risk another step. Pool Floaties reduce danger.

🐎 Horse Race

Pick a horse and watch the race. If your horse wins, you get a big payout. Golden Horseshoe boosts payout.

🔥

Hot Streak

Wins increase your streak. Losses break it unless an item protects it. Streak bonuses apply only to positive profit from a win, not refunds, break-even results, or your whole balance.

Example Win

Win $100 profit at streak 3. Hot Streak adds +20%, so you gain a $20 streak bonus.

🛡️

Lose at streak 5 with Heat Shield. Your streak becomes 4 instead of resetting to 0.

Items

Items can be general, game-specific, passive, timed, or consumed. Many break after use with an animation.

🔥Lucky Match

Protects your streak from breaking one time.

🛡️Heat Shield

A loss lowers streak by 1 instead of resetting it.

🍳Grease Fire

Next win triples Hot Streak bonus. Next loss burns 25% of current balance.

🍵Filth-Tea

Refunds a random consumed item from the current run.

⚪Weighted Ball

Queues future weighted Plinko rolls. Multiple uses stack as multiple pending rolls.

🖼️RoseKat’s Magnum Opus

For winning Plinko drops, adds 4x the profit as bonus money after the final multiplier is known.

🌺Lucky Hula Girl

Passive item. Adds extra profit to wins and stacks with copies.

💿EJM’s Record Vinyl

Gives a free Roulette spin at max bet.

🎒

Inventory Bag

Tap the bag to collapse or expand your inventory. Collapsed mode saves space on mobile.

👀

Filtered View

Only items relevant to the current game are shown. General items still appear everywhere.

🔒

Hidden ≠ Lost

Items hidden by the filter are still in your inventory. Switch games to see relevant items.

🏆

End Summary

At cash out or game over, review your graph, favorite game, most lost game, time played, best streak, Hot Streak bonus earned, and scroll through your run history.
